The Couple

Ashley and Ryan’s story began like many modern romances: with a little help from technology. After a few swipes and a handful of messages, they met for their first date over boba tea, where Ryan arrived with a slice of homemade cheesecake. It was a thoughtful and delicious gesture that made a lasting impression and quietly hinted at all the sweetness to come.

Over the course of six years, the couple has built their connection on shared adventures, both big and small. They love spending time together testing new recipes in the kitchen such as dumplings in pesto sauce and exploring new places like the local farmers market or a scenic national park. These everyday joys have become the rhythm of their relationship, turning ordinary moments into some of their most cherished memories.

When Ryan decided to propose, he went all in on sentiment and surprise. He recreated their early dates—boba, cheesecake, and all—leading Ashley on a nostalgic journey through the memories that shaped their love. At the final stop, the Museum of Fine Arts in Houston, Ryan popped the question. Spoiler alert: Ashley said “Yes!”

FAQs

Q: When should I RSVP by?

A: Please RSVP no later than December 13, 2025.

Q: Am I allowed to bring a plus one/children?

A: The couple kindly request that only those guests formally invited on the invitation attend, unless specifically indicated. In addition, while they would love to celebrate with children, the wedding will be an adults-only affair to allow for everyone to be able to relax and enjoy the night. Thank you for your understanding.

Q: What will the weather be like?

A: While the weather may vary, January in Houston typically brings mild winter weather. Guests can expect daytime temperatures ranging from the mid-50s to low 60s°F (13–17°C), with cooler evenings.

Q: Will the ceremony be outdoors or indoors?

A: Weather permitting, the ceremony will take place outdoors. In the event of inclement weather, accommodations will be made for an indoor celebration.

Q: What time should I plan to arrive?

A: The ceremony is scheduled to begin promptly at 4:00 PM. Guests are kindly requested to arrive by 3:30 PM to allow time for parking, seating, and to ensure a smooth and timely start to the celebration.

Q: Will parking be available?

A: Complimentary self-parking will be available on-site at the venue for all guests attending the celebration.

Q: Can I take photos/videos during the ceremony?

A: Please refrain from taking photos/videos during the ceremony; this will allow everyone to be fully present in the moment. A professional photographer and videographer will be capturing the event, and the couple looks forward to sharing those memories with you. There will be plenty of opportunities for engagement later on during the reception!

Q: Am I allowed to bring alcohol or smoke/vape?

A: There will be an open bar for guests to enjoy. The couple asks guests to refrain from bringing outside alcohol or smoking/vaping at the venue, in accordance with venue policies.
